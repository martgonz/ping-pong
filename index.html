Ping Pong Excitement

Ping-Pong (aka table tennis) is played by some 300 million people worldwide, according to the International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF), making it one of Earth's most popular sports. It has been an Olympic sport since 1988. Alzheimerz weekly reports a clear increase in motor skills and cognitive awareness from playing table tennis. Playing improves hand-eye coordination and it stimulates mental alertness, concentration and tactical strategy. This ping pong game is developed to provide brain stimulation for entry coding at Epicodus.

Game Rules

The ping pong game will add 1 except in the following situations

  • Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
